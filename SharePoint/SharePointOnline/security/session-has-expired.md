# "Sorry, your session has expired" when you open a document in an Office Online application

## Problem

When you try to open a document from Microsoft SharePoint Online in a Microsoft Office Online application, you receive the following error message:

- **Sorry, your session has expired. Please refresh the page to continue.**

## Solution

To resolve this issue, correct the system time, system date, or time zone on the affected computer.

## More information

This issue occurs when the date, time, or time zone is incorrect on your computer.
