# Skype Meeting Broadcast portal doesn’t load completely in the browser

## Problem

When you visit the Skype Meeting Broadcast portal at [https://portal.broadcast.skype.com](https://portal.broadcast.skype.com/) or [https://broadcast.skype.com](https://broadcast.skype.com/), you experience one of the following issues:

- The page doesn't fully load.   
- Some of the controls are missing.   
- The list of meetings isn't populated.    

## Solution

To resolve this issue, use one of the following methods, as appropriate for your situation: 

- Use InPrivate browsing in Internet Explorer or Incognito mode in Google Chrome. For more information about how to use InPrivate browsing in Internet Explorer, see the following Microsoft website:

  [Browse InPrivate in Microsoft Edge](https://windows.microsoft.com/windows/what-is-inprivate-browsing)   
- Delete your temporary Internet files. For more information, go to the following Microsoft website:

   [View and delete your browsing history in Internet Explorer](https://windows.microsoft.com/en-gb/internet-explorer/manage-delete-browsing-history-internet-explorer)   

## More Information

This issue is related to authentication behavior in the Skype Meeting Broadcast portal and to how cached files are updated.
