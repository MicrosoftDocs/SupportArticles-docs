# Documents downloaded from Teams desktop client open in protected view

## Summary

Documents downloaded from the Microsoft Teams desktop client open in protected view.

## Workaround

To workaround this issue, download the documents  from Microsoft Teams or SharePoint in a web browser. You may first have to [add the URL as a trusted location](https://support.office.com/article/Add-remove-or-change-a-trusted-location-7EE1CDC2-483E-4CBB-BCB3-4E7C67147FB4). 

## More information

Microsoft is researching this problem and will post more information in this article when it becomes available.
