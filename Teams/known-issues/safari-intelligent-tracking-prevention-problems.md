# Problems with Safari Intelligent Tracking Prevention

## Symptoms

While in preview, you may experience issues related to Safari's [Intelligent Tracking Prevention](https://support.office.com/en-us/article/safari-browser-support-1aac0a7c-35a8-42c1-a7df-f674afe234df?ui=en-US&rs=en-001&ad=US) while using Teams.

## Resolution

Teams is now available in preview on Safari 11.1+ on macOS. To mitigate issues with Intelligent Tracking Prevention while Safari browser support is in preview, go to **Preferences** > **Privacy**  and uncheck the **Prevent cross-site tracking** setting. Then, close your browser and navigate back to teams.microsoft.com in Safari.
