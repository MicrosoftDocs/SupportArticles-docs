# Can't start Teams for Surface Hub from Microsoft Store

## Symptoms

Microsoft Teams for Surface Hub won't start when started from  the Microsoft Store.

## Cause

Launching or starting the Teams for Surface Hub app from the Microsoft Store listing isn't supported by Windows on Surface Hub. 

## Resolution

Install Teams and then restart your Surface Hub.
