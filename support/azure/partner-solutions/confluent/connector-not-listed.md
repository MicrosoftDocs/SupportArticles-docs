# Confluent connector isn't in the list of connectors

This article helps you troubleshoot the sceanrio in which a Confluent connector isn't visible in the list of connectors in Azure.

## Symptoms

- A Confluent connector doesn't appear in the list of connectors.

## Causes

- The connector list might need to be refreshed.

## Solution 1: Refresh the connector list

- If you recently created a connector and it isn't in the list, select the **Refresh** button at the top of the page.

## Solution 2: Contact Confluent support

- If your Confluent connector still doesn't appear in the list, contact [Confluent support](https://support.confluent.io).
