# Troubleshoot conversations from custom channel not appearing in inbox

This article addresses the issue where conversations from a custom channel are not visible in the inbox within Customer Service workspace. It explains the cause and provides guidance for resolving this behavior.

## Prerequisites

Ensure you are using Customer Service workspace and have configured the inbox according to Microsoft's documentation.

## Potential quick workarounds

### Workaround: Verify supported inbox channels

1. Review the inbox configuration in your Customer Service workspace settings.
2. Refer to the documentation [Configure the inbox | Microsoft Learn](/dynamics365/customer-service/administer/configure-inbox) to confirm whether your channel is supported.

## Troubleshooting checklist

### Troubleshooting step

1. Ensure the custom channel is configured correctly in the Customer Service workspace.
2. Check if the custom channel is among the supported channels listed in the inbox configuration documentation.

## Cause: Custom channels are not supported for inbox

The behavior is expected because custom channels are currently not supported for inbox functionality within Customer Service workspace.

### Solution: Use supported inbox channels

1. Review the list of supported inbox channels in the documentation [Configure the inbox | Microsoft Learn](/dynamics365/customer-service/administer/configure-inbox).
2. If required, switch to a supported channel to enable inbox functionality for your conversations.

## Advanced troubleshooting and data collection

If you require additional support or have specific requirements for custom channel functionality, consider reaching out to Microsoft Support. Provide details of your current inbox configuration and the custom channel setup to facilitate further investigation.
